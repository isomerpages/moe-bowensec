<h2><strong>ICT Service Desk Services</strong></h2>
<h4><strong>Physical Location</strong></h4>
<p>D&amp;T Block C Level 4,
<br>Room C4-04 (next to student council room)
<br>Contact Number: 63859466</p>
<h4><strong>Student Visits</strong></h4>
<p>Students may visit the ICT Service Desk only during their recess, lunch
breaks or after school. Visits during lessons, CCA sessions or official
school activities are not permitted.</p>

</li>
<li>
<p>Student MIMS/SLS password resets</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Temporary loan of Personal Learning Devices (PLDs)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Re-enrolment of student PLDs</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Coordination of student PLD warranty repairs</p>
</li>
</ul>
