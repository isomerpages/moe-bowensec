<p>The Bullets Girls have been playing The Beautiful Game (joga bonito!)
in the National C and B girls' competitions since 2009.. Both B and C Divisions
have been doing well for the past years, maintaining in the top 4 position
during the National School Games(NSG). Apart from the NSG, the girls take
part in various enrichment and character building activities such as team
building camps, the FAS Referee Introductory Course, futsal competitions,
basketball, rugby, and rock climbing.</p>
