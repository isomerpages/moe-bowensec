<h3>About Bowen Drama Club</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/CCAs/Performing%20Arts/Drama%20Club/drama%20club%20pic%202.png">
</div>
<p>The Bowen Drama club has showcased the performing flair of its members
in numerous school and public performances since 1999. The Club seeks to
nurture budding talents and have them to grow and mature as stage actors.</p>
<p>Through these platforms, Drama Club members are given the opportunity
not only to showcase their talents, but also to be able to further hone
their skills and hold their own on stage.</p>
<p>Besides being groomed for stage performances, members also gain exposure
in managing the backstage work in ensuring an effective production including,
makeup skills and light and sound management skills.</p>
