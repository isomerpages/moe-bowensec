<p><strong><em>“Science is a way of thinking much more than it is a body of knowledge.”</em></strong>
</p>
<p><strong><em>~ Carl Strong</em></strong>
</p>
<p>The Bowen Science Department aims to equip students not just with scientific
knowledge and skills but also a scientific way of thinking so that students
will be able to make informed decisions about emerging science-related
issues which are important to self and society.&nbsp;</p>
<p>This is achieved through the use of the Inquiry Based approach for lessons
and activities. The conduct of inquiry is founded on three integral domains
of&nbsp;</p>
<p>a. Knowledge, Understanding and Application,
<br>b) Skills and Processes and
<br>c) Ethics and Attitudes.
<br>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:60%" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/sci%201.png">
</div>
<p>The curriculum design seeks to enable students to view the pursuit of
science as meaningful and relevant to real life.</p>
