<h2>Eco Champions</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Eco Champions in Action" src="/images/Holistic Education/Student Leadership/ecochampions.jpg">
</div>
<p>EcoChampions at Bowen Secondary School empower students to take active
ownership of sustainability and environmental stewardship.</p>
<p>Aligned with Singapore's Green Plan 2030, the programme develops informed
and responsible individuals who understand the importance of sustainable
living and are committed to making a positive impact in the school and
community.</p>
<h4>Student-Led Sustainability</h4>
<p>Through student-led initiatives, EcoChampions champion environmentally
responsible habits and inspire their peers to take collective action towards
a greener future.</p>
<h4>Learning Through Hydroponics</h4>
<p>A key feature of the programme is the school's hydroponics garden, where
students engage in hands-on learning about food security and sustainable
agriculture. Here, EcoChampions explore how food can be grown efficiently
in an urban environment while gaining practical experience in planting,
monitoring and harvesting crops. Through these experiences, students develop
an appreciation of Singapore's efforts to strengthen local food resilience
and recognise the role individuals can play in supporting these efforts.</p>
<p>The hydroponics garden also serves as a platform for meaningful community
engagement. Students connect their learning to real-world impact by using
their harvests to support community partners and outreach initiatives.
In doing so, they develop empathy, compassion and a sense of social responsibility
as they experience how their efforts can make a tangible difference in
the lives of others.</p>
<h4>Cultivating a Sustainable Future</h4>
<p>At Bowen Secondary School, the EcoChampions Programme inspires students
to move beyond awareness to meaningful action, nurturing future-ready individuals
who actively contribute to a more sustainable and caring society.</p>
