<p>Bowen Secondary School started in&nbsp;<strong>1982</strong> in the growing
new town of Ang Mo Kio. On&nbsp;**4 December 1999**, Bowen made the historic
move to our new premises at&nbsp;2 Lorong Napiri.</p>

<h3>Bowen Today</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">

<p>In January 2000, Bowen Secondary School started the new millennium as
a single session school with 1374 students.&nbsp;
<br>Bowen strives to provide a holistic and value-added education for all
Bowenians.</p>

<h3>Bowen 40<sup>th</sup> Anniversary</h3>
<p>Bowen celebrated her 4<strong>0<sup>th</sup>Anniversary in 2022</strong>.
The school logo was updated for a year to commemorate the anniversary before
reverting to the current logo in 2023.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:50%" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/About%20Bowen/Our%20Heritage/bowen%2040%20anniversary%20logo.jpg">
</div>
<p>The theme for our 40th Anniversary was <strong>BElonging</strong>, <strong>BEnefitting</strong> Lives,
and <strong>BElieving</strong>. It reflects the three things that best represent
the Bowen Experience from the time our school first opened its doors to
students, as well as the collective aspirations of our Bowen family as
we look to the future with hope and purpose.</p>
