<h3>School Fees</h3>
<p>Bowen Secondary School is a government school. The school fees tier for
2026 will be as follows:</p>
<h5>Singapore Citizens</h5>
<p>The maximum monthly fee is $25 per month.</p>
<p>Monthly fees in government or government-aided schools have 2 different
components:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>School fees are set by MOE and go towards government revenue. These fees
are different for Singapore Citizens, Singapore Permanent Residents, and
international students.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Miscellaneous fees are set by MOE but go towards individual schools’ budgets
and operating expenses. The same rate applies to all students, regardless
of nationality.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Singapore Citizens can choose to pay the miscellaneous fees from their
Edusave account by completing the online Edusave Standing Order form with
either parent's SingPass login. They may also be eligible for financial
assistance or merit-based awards and scholarships.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h5>Permanent Residents</h5>
<p>The maximum monthly fee is $700 per month.</p>
<h5>International students (ASEAN)</h5>
<p>The maximum monthly fee is $1,111.80 per month.
<br>The 11 member countries of the Association of Southeast Asian Nations
(ASEAN) are Brunei, Cambodia, Indonesia, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, the Philippines,
Singapore, Thailand, Timor-Leste, and Vietnam.</p>
<h5>International students (non-ASEAN)</h5>
<p>The maximum monthly fee is $2,211.80 per month.
<br>Examples of non-ASEAN countries include China, Japan, South Korea, Australia,
India, New Zealand, Russia, the United States, Sri Lanka, and Serbia.</p>

<h3>Payment Timeline</h3>
<p>GIRO deductions are made on the 26th of every month from February to December.
If the 26th is a weekend or public holiday, the deduction will happen on
the next working day. Check for the exact deduction dates on our calendar
by filtering for "GIRO deductions".</p>
<p>There is no deduction in January as the fees for January and February
are combined into one bill for deduction in February.</p>
<p>There will still be deductions in November and December as the fees payable
are calculated on an annual basis and spread out over a period of 12 months,
including school holidays.</p>

<h3>Student Lockers</h3>
<p>Since the implementation of the Personal Learning Device (PLD) programme,
the school has provided each student with a locker for the safe storage
of their PLD and personal belongings during the school day.</p>
<p>The lockers are located outside the respective classrooms and are assigned
according to students’ class index numbers. Each locker is fitted with
a combination lock, and students may set their own personal PIN.</p>
<p>Lockers are allocated for use within the current academic year only. Students
are required to clear and return their lockers on the last day of school.</p>
<p>Students are reminded to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Keep their lockers locked at all times</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Safeguard their personal PIN</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Use the lockers responsibly and for intended purposes only</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>The school will not be held responsible for the loss of items stored improperly.</p>
