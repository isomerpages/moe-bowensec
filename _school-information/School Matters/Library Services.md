### The Bowen School Library

<img src="/images/School-Library-Main-image.jpg" 
     style="width:60%">

The Bowen school library is a purposeful place that houses and offers a selection of hardcopy books and resources in different languages.. When students step into the library, they are often greeted by the attractive thematic displays throughout the year.

<img src="/images/library%202.png" 
     style="width:70%">
		 
Spending time in the school library is a great way for students to lessen screen time and drive away digital fatigue. It also provides a quiet and conducive place for students who wish to focus and study.

### Online Public Access Catalogue (OPAC)

<img src="/images/OPAC%20Bowensec.png" 
     style="width:60%">

The  Online Public Access Catalogue (OPAC) allows you to search through the existing school library resources and find the specific item you are looking for.. You can access OPAC at home, in school and using  your smartphones.     
  
OPAC Link: [https://schoolibrary.moe.edu.sg/bowensec](https://schoolibrary.moe.edu.sg/bowensec)

### 'MySchoolLibrary' Mobile App

<img src="/images/MySchoolLibrary%20App.png" 
     style="width:35%">
		 
MySchoolLibrary mobile app is your anywhere 24/7 library! Just like your School OPAC, you can access your School OPAC on this app as well. You can download the app for free on your iPhone or Android phone.   
  

These are the functions available on the mobile app 

1.  Search for books & e-books, check item availability and place reservations;
2.   View loans, reservations, fines and charges;
3.  Search and register for library events;
4.  Save list of favourite titles; and
5.  “Discover” book recommendations, featured items, new arrivals and books others have read.

### Instructions for downloading and installing the Mobile Application.

Here are the instructions for downloading and installing the Mobile Application.  

1.  Scan the QR code (Android or Apple)
2.  Search for ‘My SchoolLibrary’ 
3.  Tap on install 
4.  Login with: T0XXXXXC (BC number)
